Tips

1. Only buy a pet if you have proper space, proper time to spare, proper facilities with you all ready.

2. Do not keep a pet just for fun assuming that it will survive.

3. Always keep your pet away from predators and insects which can cause harm.

4. You should give a nice bath to your pet every month (if you will give it bath regularly, it can be sick).

5. In case on reproducing, take extra care of your pet or give your pet to the recommended vet.

6. Remember, ALL PETS NEED AMPLE AMOUNT OF SPACE TO RUN, EXERCISE, PLAY ETC.
